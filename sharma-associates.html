Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to questions we hear most often from clients across Uttar Pradesh.

What happens during a free consultation?
+
During your free consultation, a senior advocate will listen to the facts of your matter, explain your legal position honestly, and outline the available options. There is no obligation to engage our services, and everything discussed is completely confidential.
How much does it cost to hire Sharma & Associates?
+
Our fees vary by matter type and complexity. We provide a clear, written fee estimate before any work begins — no hidden charges. For personal injury cases, we typically operate on a no-win, no-fee basis. Contact us for a free initial assessment.
How long does a typical case take to resolve?
+
Timelines vary significantly by matter. Simple contracts or documentation can be completed in days. Civil disputes in district courts typically take 6–18 months. High Court appeals may take longer. We will give you a realistic estimate during your consultation based on the specifics of your case.
Do you handle cases outside Lucknow?
+
Yes. We practice across Uttar Pradesh in all District Courts, the Allahabad High Court (Lucknow Bench and Allahabad), and the Supreme Court of India. Our New Delhi office handles matters in Delhi courts and national tribunals. We also advise NRI clients internationally.
Is my information kept confidential?
+
Absolutely. All communications between you and our advocates are protected by advocate-client privilege under the Bar Council of India Rules. Your information will never be shared with any third party without your explicit consent.
Can I get legal help in Hindi?
+
हाँ, बिल्कुल। हमारे सभी अधिवक्ता हिंदी और अंग्रेज़ी दोनों में सेवाएं प्रदान करते हैं। हम यह सुनिश्चित करते हैं कि आप अपनी भाषा में अपनी कानूनी स्थिति को पूरी तरह समझें। Yes — all our advocates are fluent in Hindi and English, and we ensure every client fully understands their legal position in their preferred language.

Adv. Rajiv Sharma founded this firm in Civil Lines, Lucknow in 1998 with two associates and a single guiding principle: that every client, regardless of their means, deserves honest counsel and vigorous representation.
